Our Story

When Marcus arrived at Laurentian Hills Christian School in grade 7, he stood out to Andrea because he was the new kid. To Marcus, though, Andrea was just another face in the crowd. Neither of them could guess where they both would be nearly 9 years later. In high school, after catching each other's eyes at a badminton tournament hosted by the Waterloo Christian Reformed Church, they struck up a conversation on Facebook based on a mutual interest in Doctor Who. A few months later, Andrea invited Marcus to her 17th birthday party at the Plattsville Arena, where Marcus had to try to put his best foot forward in his Great Uncle Gustav's 50 year old skates. They decided to keep seeing each other though, so that must not have gone too badly.

Two proms, and two high school graduation ceremonies later, Marcus and Andrea both made it to university. Marcus was off to Wilfrid Laurier University for Computing and Computer Electronics and Andrea to the University of Waterloo in Systems Design Engineering. Though school was (and still is) hectic, they were able to enjoy many lunches and study sessions together, their campuses only separated by a short walk. During co-op terms in Toronto, Andrea would take the train home every weekend so they could spend more time together.

On January 2, 2017, 3 years after the skating party, Marcus took Andrea out for dinner and then skating at an arena he had rented just for the two of them. About halfway through their skate, Marcus knelt down and proposed on the ice! They were congratulated by the zamboni driver on their way out. Both of them are very excited to continue their lives together and to pledge their commitment to each other on their wedding day, April 28, 2018.
